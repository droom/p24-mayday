Chris Droom is an old man with breathing problems. He is very into lawn care.

I've worked professionally in graphic design for 17+ years. Over this time have accrued a wide and varied range of skills. I have a careful, reflective practice, grounded in the wider design canon, with a particular interest in typography. I am a good listener, and I seek to create welcoming, generative environments for ideas to be nurtured. In 2019, I co-founded a digital co-operative—Good Praxis—embedding our values of dignity at work, positive social impact and democracy in the workplace. I'm currently working independently with clients, but am on the look out for a permanent position ideally within the Arts, education, or impact sectors. I have two boys, Joel (9) & Kiran (14 months).
