About Chris Droom

I have worked professionally in graphic communication since 2006 and over this time have accrued a wide and varied range of skills. I am a good listener, and I seek to create welcoming, generative environments for ideas to be nurtured. I have a careful, reflective practice, grounded in the wider design canon.

In 2019, I co-founded a digital co-operative—Good Praxis—embedding our values of dignity at work, positive social impact and democracy in the workplace.

I have two boys, Joel (9) & Kiran (14 months).
