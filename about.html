I have a careful, reflective practice, grounded in the wider design canon. I have worked in graphic communication since 2006 and have accrued a wide range of skills—from implementing brands to art to creating motion graphics. I’ve previously co-founded a digital co-operative—Good Praxis—embedding our values of dignity at work, positive social impact and democracy in the workplace. I am working to become a member of The International Society of Typographic Designers. I am a good listener, and I seek to create welcoming, generative environments for ideas to be nurtured. I have been drawing letters and logos since I was a child (before I knew the terms typography or branding). I started designing websites aged 15 using MS Frontage. 22 years on, I'm still as passionate about it as I was in '98. I have grown up with the web, being at the forefront of major design advancements, including CSS, responsive design, Flexbox/Grid, to name a few. I have a particular interest in typography, design systems, and branding. I have also learnt a lot from other designers who have been really patient and generous with their time. I primarily work in Figma, but am proficient with the entire Adobe CC suite. I have two boys, Joel (8) & Kiran (9 months)
